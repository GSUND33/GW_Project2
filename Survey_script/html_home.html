Mission Satement

Every day billions upon billions of data points are created around the world. From every sales transaction to tweets and posts, even where we travel and what we search online is data that gets collected and stored. All of this data tells different stories about life on earth. In order to convey this information we often rely on visualizing it with charts, graphs, maps, and other graphic depictions. However all data is not the same and as such not every story can be told with just any visualization. It’s exactly this point which we hope to explore by collecting data on how people best understand the message different visualizations are trying to convey.


Please be a part of our exploration by taking and sharing our quiz.
